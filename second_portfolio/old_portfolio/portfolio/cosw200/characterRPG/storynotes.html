Locations

Neighborhood

The disordered society where the story takes place.

Miasma

A hazy town located on the northwestern coast of Neighborhood. Towering smokestacks spew exhaust into the sky, scattered accross the landscape. The majority of the coast is composed of cliffs. Many elevators travel to the base of the cliffs where broad ports receive small and curious cargo ships. Down here the haze is minimal but hovers just meters above most lampposts.

Helotry

A nightly town located on the coast of Neighborhood's southereastern peninsula. It's mountainside is plastered with homes glowing their tiny lights after sunsent. A place renown for it's seemingly limitless lawlessness. The line between friend or foe is unclear.

Haulm

A remote and cold town located on the northeastern coast of Neighborhood. It's soft blankets of snow are more of a hindrance than they are enjoyable. The town itself is quiet and fairly unlike the rest of the island's populations. There is a higher sense of trust and civility. Watchposts align the mountainsides like airplane hangers. They can be seen at night via torches.

Antimony

A vast coastal town extending into cave systems within its great cliffsides located on the eastern coast of Neighborhood. Although it isn't as lawless as Helotry it still experiences a great deal of mischevous activity.

Ossuary

A great graveyard for the misfortunate located inland on the southwestern peninsula of Neighborhood. It's has a bleak greenish atmostphere but manages to be littered with vibrant flowers. The overseers roam throughout the lands with their elongated necks and flash-light like eyes.

Vanity

A regal town in the heart of Neighborhood. Neighborhood's command resides. A small manor houses Neighborhood's rulers.

Abilities (Classes)

Lying & Manipulation (Misleader/Deceiver)

Lying allows easier collection of information and items.
Manipulation greatly increases the chance people will help you although only for a limited amount times.

Dual Personalities & Secrecy (Pretender/Performer)

Dual Personalities gives the chance people will collect false information.
Secrecy prevents people from collecting information.

Honesty & Helpfulness (Ethicist/the Just)

Honesty greatly increases the chance people provide information but it's validity is uncertain.
Helpfulness increases the chance people provide items.

Forgiveness & Compassion (Saint/Altruist)

Forgiveness increases the chance people will help you (Not as effiecient as manipulation but allows limitless aid).
Compassion increases the chance people provide information.

Illnesses & Disorders (Races)

Annxiety - Affects rate at which all things are completed in. Prevents some things from being done without knowing required information.

Depression - Affects chances things are done correctly. Prevents most things from being done without help.

Depersonalization Disorder - Affects chances of gathering new information from others. Prevents some interpersonal bonds from forming.

Narcolepsy - Affects rate at which all things are completed in. Prevents some information from being retained.

Character Statistics

Anger - Affects chance to automatically select aggressive responses.

Awkwardness - Affects chance to automatically prevent dialogue from starting.

Disgust - Affects chance to automatically remove responses that lead to immoral actions.

Envy - Affects chance of automatically stealing items and affects Anger.

Fear - Affects chance of things being done quickly.

Happiness - Affects chance of automatically selecting responses that lead to moral actions.

Love - Affects chance of befriending others and gaining information. (Positvely as opposed to Pride)

Pride - Affects chance of befriending others and gaining information (Negatively as opposed to Love). Affects chance of automatically doing immoral actions to gain benefits.

Sadness - Affects chances of automatically selecting responses that lead to difficult actions.

Shame - Affects chance to recover from immoral actions and raise positive stats.

Stress - Affects chance to raise all negative stats.

Stoicism - Affects chance to lower all stats.
