Anger

Affects chance to automatically select aggressive responses.

Awkwardness

Affects chance to automatically prevent dialogue from starting.

Disgust

Affects chance to automatically remove responses that lead to immoral actions.

Envy

Affects chance of automatically stealing items and affects Anger.

Fear

Affects chance of things being done quickly.

Happiness

Affects chance of automatically selecting responses that lead to moral actions.

Love

Affects chance of befriending others and gaining information. (Positvely as opposed to Pride)

Pride

Affects chance of befriending others and gaining information (Negatively as opposed to Love). Affects chance of automatically doing immoral actions to gain benefits.

Sadness

Affects chances of automatically selecting responses that lead to difficult actions.

Shame

Affects chance to recover from immoral actions and raise positive stats.

Stoicism

Affects chance to lower all stats.

Stress

Affects chance to raise all negative stats.
